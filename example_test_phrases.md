# Example Test Phrases for Emotion Detection

## Happy/Positive
1. "I just got promoted at work and I'm so excited! This is the best day of my life!"
2. "I'm feeling grateful for all the amazing people in my life. Everything is wonderful!"
3. "Just finished my project and I'm so proud of what I accomplished!"

## Sad/Depression
1. "I feel so empty inside. Nothing seems to matter anymore and I can't find joy in anything."
2. "I've been crying all day and I don't even know why. I just feel hopeless."
3. "I feel like a burden to everyone around me. Maybe they'd be better off without me."

## Anxious/Stressed
1. "I can't stop worrying about tomorrow's presentation. My heart is racing and I can't sleep."
2. "Everything feels overwhelming. I have so many deadlines and I don't know how to handle it all."
3. "I keep having panic attacks and I'm afraid to leave my house. What if something bad happens?"

## Angry/Frustrated
1. "I'm so furious right now! How could they treat me like this after everything I've done?"
2. "This is absolutely unacceptable! I can't believe this is happening again!"
3. "I'm fed up with all the lies and betrayal. I can't take it anymore!"

## Mixed Emotions
1. "I'm excited about the new job but terrified I won't be good enough. What if I fail?"
2. "I love my family but sometimes I feel so trapped and suffocated by their expectations."
3. "I'm proud of how far I've come but sad about what I had to give up to get here."

## Love/Caring
1. "I'm so grateful to have such amazing friends who always support me through everything."
2. "Being with my partner makes me feel so loved and appreciated. I'm the luckiest person."
3. "Seeing my children grow and learn brings me so much joy and pride every single day."

## Fear/Nervousness
1. "I'm terrified of what the doctor might say. I can't stop thinking about worst case scenarios."
2. "What if I mess up the interview? I'm so nervous I feel sick to my stomach."
3. "I keep having nightmares and I'm afraid to go to sleep. The fear is overwhelming."

Then copy and paste any of these examples to see how the AI analyzes different emotions!
